    Hi...

    ...My name is Fabien. I'm a student at the Institut Polytechnique de Paris and at Télécom Paris. I'm currently in my final year of the engineering cycle and in the Master 2 SETI program.

    In particular, I'm a passionate student of embedded systems and FPGAs. Prior to that, I studied quantum, distributed computing and high-performance computing in the Quantum and Distributed Computer Science master's program at Université Paris-Saclay, with a preference for distributed computing. 😃

    After many long and exciting discussions and fond memories of my ancient embedded systems projects, I decided to focus my studies on this field.

    It was not long before I started spending my time on FPGAs and embedded systems. I was hooked. Today, I'd like to continue in this field to be able to innovate and optimize complex systems.

    In the past, I did what's called “Travaux d'Étude et de Recherche” at the Université Paris-Saclay on the subject of agreement between mobile agents, and then a research internship with Sebastian Brandt on distributed coloring.

    While we're on the subject of distributed computing and embedded systems, you can find on this website a handful of tutos, tips and even courses (from what I've read or learned). Not only about distributed computing and embedded systems, but about any subject that interests me. I'll likely write about lambda-calculus, graph theory, quantum computing...

    I think that's about it! Have fun reading the articles on this site! 😄
