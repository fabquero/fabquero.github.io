    Hi...

    ...My name is Fabien. I am a student at the University of Paris-Saclay. I am currently in the Magister degree of computer science and in the first year of my master's degree. More precisely, I study quantum computer science, distributed computer science, and high-performance computing in the master's degree called: Quantum and distributed computer science.

    At first, I chose this master's degree for the quantum computing part. I was particularly interested in this field after reading quantum books. Nowadays, I have finally discovered a passion for distributed computer science.

    Thus, I have decided to head for this domain. I have started a "Travaux d'Etude et de Recherche" at the University of Paris-Saclay about the agreement with mobile agents. Now, I'm doing a research internship with Sebastian Brandt on distributed coloring.

    Talking about distributed computer science, you can find on this webpage some tutorials, tips, or even some courses (from my reading or my courses). It will be about distributed computer science but also about all the subjects for which I have an interest. I could write about lambda calculus, graph theory, and quantum computer science...

    Okay, I think that's enough about me. Have fun reading the articles on this website. 😄
